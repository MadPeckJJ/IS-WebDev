Golf.

Description

Golf is my favorite hobby. I love golf because it not only drives my competitive nature, it is calming and acts as a sanctuary for my mind.
The idea of golf is silly, just put the ball in the hole. However, the game is far more complex than many think.
Golf is often seen as one of the most difficult sports in the world, and I would know. I have played for four years now and I am not even near being considered a "scratch" golfer.
That to me is what makes it so fun, so satisfying. There is nothing better than hitting a pure shot, considering the difficulty of it. On top of that the sounds of the game are unmatched in any other sport.
The sounds of nature in the distance, the sound of a pure iron shot, the sound of the ball hitting the bottom of the cup. To me, there's nothing else like it.



The thing I love most about golf is seeing all the different courses.
